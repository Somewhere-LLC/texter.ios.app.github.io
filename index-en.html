What you can do

Voice, images, videos—every format becomes searchable text.

Studio-grade voice to text

Powered by Whisper (OpenAI) and other top engines. Auto punctuation and long-form handling keep transcripts clear and accurate.

OCR for images & video

Extract text from photos in a tap. Pull audio from video files and convert it to text without extra tools.

Live translations

Works with DeepL and Google Translate so you can read and share transcripts in the language you need, instantly.

AI summaries & smart lists

Get meeting highlights automatically, organize notes with folders and search, and stay on top of every conversation.

Silence removal

Auto-detect and remove silent segments from audio and video. Three preset levels (light, standard, strong) for fine-tuning.

Video subtitles

Auto-generate timestamped captions for imported videos. Edit and play back sentence by sentence with one tap.

Built for work, learning, and accessibility

Texter is more than a recorder—it’s a quiet assistant that helps you stay present while everything is captured for later.

  • Meetings: Capture every point, auto-punctuated and ready to summarize.
  • Writers & creators: Dictate ideas, interviews, or scripts and move straight to editing.
  • Accessibility: Live captions support clear communication in any setting.
  • Language study: Practice pronunciation and turn PDFs or slides into editable text.
